Black-Scholes Pricing Model

The Black-Scholes model is a mathematical formula used to estimate the fair price of European-style options. It helps traders understand how much an option should cost based on factors like time, volatility, and interest rates. Think of it as a tool that brings logic and consistency to pricing bets on future market movements.
